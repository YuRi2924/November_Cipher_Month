ABOUT THIS GAME

The November Cipher is a mystery puzzle adventure game crafted by Yuri Jimenez, president of the Artificial Universe Guild. Set in an eerie atmosphere inspired by the unsettling worlds of Junji Ito, players engage in a core gameplay loop of solving four cryptic challenges, each designed to test their critical thinking and puzzle-solving skills. The objective is to unravel the mysteries hidden within each puzzle, leading to a final truth. This single-player experience ensures that only one victor will emerge, relying solely on their wits to navigate the dark suspenseful journey ahead.

GAME MECHANICS

Theres is a series of four cryptic ciphers awaits you. Each one holds a key, a fragment of a final, elusive four-digit code. Your mission is to unravel all four secrets and present the complete code to the UC STEM Society page. Only then will the truth be revealed. The game master watches closely, ready to verify your success. Clues will be released each week, but beware—decoding them is no simple task.

GAME REWARDS

This game is a race against time, shrouded in secrecy. It operates on a first-come, first-served basis. Only those who can swiftly uncover the four missing codes will secure their victory and claim the following prizes:

  • JUNJI ITO TALES OF MACABRE STICKERS
  • STEM SOC ID LACE
  • AUG KEYCHAIN
  • ID PIN
